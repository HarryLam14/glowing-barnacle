Artist Showcase

Emma Brownjohn

Emma Brownjohn, illustrator-turned-painter, has a deft use of colour and harmonious composition. Brownjohn finds inspiration in personal experiences, whether a line of a poem or a glimpse of a fluorescent jacket in a grey city. Beautiful and charming, the works capture fleeting moments in time. Explore more about Emma's work in her artist spotlight.

Klimt

Influential Art Nouveau artist Gustav Klimt (1862-1918) became known for his sensual figures against sumptuously patterned backdrops, most famously in his iconic 'The Kiss'. Our collection also includes Klimt's paintings of landscapes and gardens from his travels around Austria and Italy, showing luminous light and tones.
